Character Design

December 2024

Meet my latest creation: a bold, electrifying character designed to light up the stage in any game universe. This punk-rock guitarist blends a rebellious edge with a vibrant and memorable aesthetic, making them the perfect fit for action-packed adventures or lighthearted, personality-driven games. The character’s spiked neon hair and fiery color palette scream energy and creativity, while their leather jacket and combat boots anchor them with a gritty, no-nonsense attitude. The custom electric guitar isn’t just an accessory—it’s a weapon of personality, a reflection of the character’s dynamic spirit. This is a hero (or anti-hero) built to resonate with players who crave excitement, humor, and a connection to a world that doesn’t take itself too seriously. In designing this character, I drew inspiration from the vibrant universes of Fortnite, Rayman Legends, and Worms, aiming for a mix of iconic appeal and limitless adaptability. The character’s exaggerated features and expressive stance make them an immediate standout, but they’re also versatile enough to feel at home in a fast-paced multiplayer brawl, a chaotic platformer, or even a story-driven indie game with a comedic twist. This design is all about creating a sense of fun, individuality, and energy—an invitation for players to step into a game world that feels alive and buzzing with possibility. I’ve aimed for a visual language that speaks directly to what makes your games so captivating: bold design choices, accessible creativity, and a love for characters that players won’t forget.
